About AskBlueJay.ai

AskBlueJay.ai is the AI-powered merchant assistant created by Fortified Capital LLC to help small business owners save big on credit card processing. Founded in 2025, our mission is to eliminate hidden fees and overpriced systems by offering smarter, faster tools powered by real-time savings analysis.

From retail to mobile vendors, AskBlueJay helps identify better rates, streamline signups, and connect merchants with trusted solution programs. Built for simplicity, designed for results.
